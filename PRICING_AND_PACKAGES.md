# VenueBrief Pricing and Packages

## Current value stage

VenueBrief is not yet a full SaaS company. It is a validated lightweight operational tool with real-world testing.

## Recommended pricing ladder

### Free Demo
For initial interest.
- Neutral sample venue
- Fake staff
- Custom stations demo
- Sample printable brief

Purpose: get people to understand the product.

### Early Access - £19/month per venue
For small operators who can self-serve.
- Access to current stable demo/build
- Use on one venue
- No custom implementation included

Purpose: validate willingness to pay.

### Setup Pack - £99 one-off
For operators who want their venue configured.
- Custom station setup
- Staff role template
- Sample shift brief
- Short usage guide

Purpose: simple paid pilot without SaaS obligations.

### Supported Pilot - £149 setup + £29/month
For serious small venues.
- Custom setup
- Updates to template
- Basic support
- Early feature feedback

Purpose: first commercial product shape.

## What not to sell yet

Do not sell:
- payroll;
- HR compliance;
- time tracking;
- live multi-user SaaS;
- API integrations;
- enterprise security;
- legal compliance guarantees.

## Best current offer

**£99 setup one-off** or **£19/month per venue early access**.

This validates whether people will pay without forcing a full SaaS build.
