## Decision on cinematic concept

The cinematic concept is not promoted to homepage.

Reason:
- It is visually interesting but less clear on mobile.
- It feels more tech/AI than hospitality operations.
- The current premium homepage communicates the product faster.

Keep it as a separate concept only.

## Freeze rule

No more design changes until at least 3-5 external hospitality contacts review the landing and answer:

1. Do you understand what VenueBrief does within 10 seconds?
2. Would this help you run a shift?
3. Would you pay £19/month or £99 setup?
4. What would stop you from using it?
5. What is missing before you would test it in your venue?

## Product positioning

VenueBrief is not a rota builder, payroll system, HR platform or compliance tool.

It turns an existing rota into a clear operational shift brief: staff, stations, handovers, warnings and print-ready output.
