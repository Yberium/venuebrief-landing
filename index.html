The problem

Rotas show who is working. They do not always show who owns each station, when handovers happen or what needs checking before service.

The solution

VenueBrief converts the daily staff setup into a clear operational plan with stations, warnings and a print-ready brief.

The fit

Built for restaurants, wine bars, hospitality venues, events teams and small operators who need clarity without heavy software.

What it does

  • Daily staff setup
  • Venue-specific custom stations
  • Station ownership and handover visibility
  • Warning-based review without blocking the shift
  • Clean print/PDF output
  • No login, no backend and no heavy implementation

VenueBrief does not replace your rota system. It turns your rota into an operational plan your team can actually use.
