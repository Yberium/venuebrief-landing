## Problem

Most rotas tell you who is working.
They do not always show:

- who owns each station;
- when handovers happen;
- which areas need attention;
- what the manager should check before service;
- whether the final brief is clear enough for the team.

That gap creates confusion during busy shifts.

## Solution

VenueBrief gives managers a lightweight way to build a practical shift brief from the rota:

- add staff for the day;
- set start and finish times;
- add venue-specific stations;
- assign cover;
- review warnings;
- print a clean shift brief.

No login. No backend. No complex setup.
