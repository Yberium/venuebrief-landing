# Yberium Pulse landing rebrand

This branch implements the approved rebrand from VenueBrief to **Yberium Pulse**.

## Locked brand decisions

- Brand: Yberium
- Product: Yberium Pulse
- Tagline: **Know the shift before it starts.**
- Positioning: Shift readiness and operational control for hospitality teams.
- Primary domain: `yberium.com`

## Approved visual system

- Graphite `#171A1F`
- Signal Lime `#B7F34A`
- Operational Green `#2FBF71`
- Amber `#F4B740`
- Critical Red `#E05252`
- Off White `#F5F6F7`

## Landing scope

1. New Yberium Pulse header and identity.
2. Hero copy and CTAs aligned to the approved mockup.
3. Product dashboard presentation covering readiness, coverage, risks and handover.
4. Module strip: Pulse Brief, Pulse Relay, Pulse Handover, Pulse Review, Pulse Control.
5. Benefits and how-it-works sections.
6. Mobile-first responsive implementation.
7. Metadata and canonical preparation for `yberium.com`.

## Guardrails

- Do not alter operational logic in the application repositories.
- Keep legacy URLs working until the domain cutover is tested.
- Do not silently reintroduce VenueBrief public branding.
- The full source-of-truth plan and official visual references are stored in Google Drive under `Yberium Pulse`.
